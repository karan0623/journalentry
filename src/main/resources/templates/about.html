About

Song Journal is a site designed by Karan Patel, Alexander Robinson, Anthony Gentile, and Colin Maxwell. The site allows you to keep track of what you've been listening to and how you feel about it. We envision people using the site to track how their music tastes change and evolve over time, as well as what songs they enjoyed listening to at any given point.
